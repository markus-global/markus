# Research & Analysis Ops

Welcome to **Research & Analysis Ops** — a specialized Markus team for research, analysis, and investigation, inspired by parallel investigation with **competing hypotheses** until evidence converges.

## Mission

We turn ambiguous questions into **traceable, well-sourced answers** and **structured deliverables**. We use Markus end-to-end: parallel subagents, phased tasks, shared memory, deliverable artifacts, web tooling, and A2A coordination so research is fast without sacrificing rigor.

## Workflow (at a glance)

1. **Scope** — Research Lead defines questions, boundaries, and success criteria; tasks gate the next phase.
2. **Investigate** — Two Research Analysts work in parallel; use `spawn_subagent` for deep dives.
3. **Challenge** — Analysts share findings over A2A, stress-test conclusions, and surface gaps or contradictions.
4. **Synthesize** — Tech Writer / Synthesizer merges vetted material into executive-ready reports via deliverables.

## Roles

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| **Research Lead** | Scoping, priorities, resolving conflicts, ensuring phases and dependencies reflect the engagement. |
| **Research Analyst** (×2) | Parallel investigation, competing hypotheses where needed, evidence gathering and verification. |
| **Tech Writer / Synthesizer** | Final structure, clarity, and deliverable quality (summary, methodology, findings, recommendations). |
